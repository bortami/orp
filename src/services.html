Race & Finish Line Photography

From 5Ks to full marathons, I offer professional photography services focused on endurance events across Oklahoma. Whether you're a race director looking for reliable finish line coverage or a runner wanting to relive race day, I capture high-quality, bib-tagged photos with fast turnaround and simple delivery.

What to Expect

Every event is different, but here's the standard process I follow for coverage:

  • I scout the location in advance or work with your race map to identify best spots
  • On race day, I arrive early and capture candid, dynamic, and finish line moments
  • Photos are uploaded and bib-tagged
  • You’ll receive a gallery link within 12–48 hours
  • Sponsors can be highlighted with custom watermarks or overlays

Why Work With Oklahoma Race Photos?

I’m not a studio photographer moonlighting at races- I specialize in endurance events and know how to capture the moments that matter. You’ll get someone who understands race timing, logistics, and what runners and race directors really care about. Whether you're hosting a community 5K or a sponsored half marathon, I’ve got you covered with reliable, fast, and personal photo coverage.

  1. Specialized in Races
    I focus exclusively on Oklahoma endurance events—nothing else.
  2. Bib-tagged Photos
    Every image is tagged using AI to make it easy for runners to find themselves.
  3. Sponsor-Ready Overlays
    Deliver real value to your sponsors with branded galleries and image watermarks.
  4. One Point of Contact
    No outsourcing or teams—you deal directly with me, every step of the way.

Transparent Flexible Pricing

Participant-Paid Coverage

This is the most hands-off option for race organizers. I attend your event, shoot, edit, tag the photos, and make them available for runners to purchase directly. There’s no cost to you as the race director.

You book me and provide race details
I show up early and photograph the start, finish line, and on-course action
All images are bib-tagged using Tagily and uploaded to my website
Runners can search by bib number and purchase digital downloads or physical prints
You receive a link to the public gallery to share with your participants

You get a link to the gallery to distribute, but do not receive image files or usage rights unless purchased separately.

Best For: Small to mid-size races with tight budgets or those who want to provide photo access without upfront costs.

Flat-Rate Race Coverage
This package gives you full access to the photo set and usage rights for promotion. Runners can still access the gallery, but you, the race organizer, receive all images to use however you’d like.
You book and pay a flat fee before race day
I cover your event as agreed—start, finish, crowd, branded signage, etc.
Images are edited, bib-tagged, and uploaded to your gallery
You receive a download link to the entire set of high-resolution photos
You can share them with runners, post on social, or use in future promotions

You receive non-exclusive, perpetual rights to use all delivered photos for social media, your race website, printed materials, and email newsletters, excluding commercial resale or third-party licensing without a separate commercial license.

Best For: Races that want marketing content and full access to their images without limiting access to participants.

Premium Sponsor Coverage
This package is designed for races with sponsors who want branded exposure. All images are overlaid with sponsor logos and optimized for shareability. You receive the full gallery plus promotional deliverables.
I meet with you in advance to coordinate sponsor branding and delivery goals
I photograph your race with emphasis on capturing sponsor elements (banners, shirts, archways)
All images are bib-tagged and branded with your sponsor's watermark or overlay
A highlight set is edited for social media and sent same-day if requested
Full gallery is uploaded and shared with you and your sponsor(s)

You receive perpetual, non-exclusive commercial rights to use all photos for race promotion, sponsor recap, and publication across digital and print channels, with sponsors permitted to use branded images for advertising and social media.

Best For:Events with sponsor contracts, large community visibility, or those needing fast turnaround and polished media assets.
